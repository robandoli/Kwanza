Capoeira is a practice that cannot sustain itself without memory. Every movement, every berimbau rhythm, every song carries the experience transmitted by generations of masters and capoeiristas who came before us.

## Respect for Lineage

At Grupo Kwanza, we understand that ancestry is not merely a reference to the past, but a living presence in the present. When we enter the roda, we bring with us the teachings of Mestre Zambi, Mestre Gato Preto, Mestre Leopoldina, and so many others who dedicated their lives to capoeira.
